Course Engineer

Vienna, Austria

Description

At Mimo, we’re teaching hundreds of thousands of people how to code in a way that fits into their everyday lives. To build and expand our game-like courses on app-building, website-making, hacking, and more, we’re looking for a full-time Course Engineer to join the Mimo crew.

High-quality content forms the core of our app and is the very reason why so many people learn with us. As Course Engineer, your mission will be to draft and write exciting courses in our course builder while also improving existing courses based on analytics and feedback.


In a nutshell, you’ll be in charge of:
  • Building fresh courses
  • Maintaining and expanding existing courses
  • Researching best practices
  • Coming up with course ideas and testing them

Requirements

You’ll be successful if you:
  • Can explain things very well
  • Have programming and software development experience
  • Can and want to learn additional programming languages and technologies all the time
  • Have experience in and a talent for writing


You can expect competitive compensation between 40,000€ - 50,000€. If we’ve captured your interest, apply now and let us know why you’re the perfect fit for the job.
